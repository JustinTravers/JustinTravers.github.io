  • Ty, the protagonist, is the heroic Tasmanian tiger who is controlled by the player on the mission to rescue his family from what is called the dreamtime.
  • Boss Cas, a cassowary is the main antagonist attempting to become the ruler using the five talismans power.
  • Julius is a koala scientist who aids Ty in his search for the five talismans, and also crafts more boomerangs for ty to use on his journey.
  • Ken, a Tasmanian devil is a park ranger in the ------- level.
  • Dennis is a green tree frog other than that he is useless.
  • Shazza, who is a female dingo is Ty’s love interest.
  • Maurie is a sulphur-crested cockatoo who acts as a guide throughout the story and is Ty’s mentor figure.
